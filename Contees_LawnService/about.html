Our Story

Contee's Lawn Service was built on a simple idea: people deserve a lawn care company that actually shows up, does great work, and charges a fair price. No runaround, no surprise fees, no flaking on appointments.

We're a locally owned and operated business right here in Lubbock, TX. That means we're invested in this community — your neighbors are our neighbors, and your yard is a reflection of the neighborhood we all share.

Whether it's a routine mow, a seasonal cleanup, or reclaiming a yard that's gotten out of control, we bring the same level of care and professionalism to every single job.
